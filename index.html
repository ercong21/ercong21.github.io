Hi there, I'm Ercong Nie!

I'm best known as the horrible cop from A Touch of Evil. Don't trust me. Read more about my life...

I am a second year Master student of computational linguistics at the Center for Information and Language Processing at LMU Munich. Besides, I study informatics at Institut of Informatics as minor subject.

Previously, I completed my B.A. degree in German linguistics and Finance at the School of Foreign Languages at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). I spent a semester during my bachelor study as an exchange student at Institut für Deutsch als Fremdsprachenphilologie, University of Heidelberg, where I studied Germanistik im Kulturvergleich.
